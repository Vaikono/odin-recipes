Steak Recipe

A steak is a thick cut of meat. It is normally grilled or fried. It also can be diced, cooked in sauce, or minced and formed into patties, such as hamburgers.

On this page, you will learn how to make steak on the grill.

Ingredient:

  • Meat cuts that are at least 1-inch thick
  • Marinade and rubs

Steps:

  1. If steak was marinated, wipe off the wet marinade with paper towel
  2. Else, set the meat on the hot grill and leave it alone for several minutes
  3. Sear steaks over high, direct heat, then move them to indirect heat
  4. Once the steak is done to your liking, remove it from the grill.
  5. Let your steak rest for at least 5 minutes
  6. Enjoy!

How to test your steak doneness

  • Rare -- 125 degrees F (52 degrees C)
  • Medium Rare -- 130 degrees F (54 degrees C)
  • Medium -- 140 degrees F (60 degrees C)
  • Medium Well Well -- 150 degrees F (65 degrees C)
  • Well done -- 160 degrees F (70 degrees C)
